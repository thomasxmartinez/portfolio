Some on this review write, "taste like fresh squeezed lemons", "best ever", "prefect tasting." My view point from being a former Lemonade King, may taste like fresh squeezed from foreign lemons, but not American grown. "Best ever", what an irony when that most likely never tasted my lemonade, or even lemonade downtown at many local stores, that they never knew sold lemonade. "Perfect tasting", what are you nuts! Nobody likes heartburn in their throats, and this is the perfect lemonade that causes such, why - the product has too much of an ingredient that isn't pleasing to adults, I would be willing to say if the product was placed in a metal container most likely would begin to leak within the week because of the high content of that acidic ingredient. The real palate taste remained me from drinking directly from one of those plastic yellow shaped lemon concentrated item found in the produce section of most stores, not 100% lemon juice without the other added ingredients. When noted 100%, means no other ingredients; and, if Milo's thinks this is the best they should ask those who've worked at Milo's if they are whom they claim behind closed doors. Stay away from words, drink what is good for you, not good for the producer - Milo's isn't it......
